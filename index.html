Camila Zárate-Guerén

Hi! I am a third year PhD student at the University of Birmingham under the supervision of Richard Mycroft.

Previously, I received my Master's degree from Universidad de Chile in 2022, with Maya Stein as my advisor.

My research interests lie in combinatorics, specifically in graph theory. During my master's thesis I worked in extremal graph theory and digraphs. Lately, I have been also working on spanning structures in hypergraphs.
